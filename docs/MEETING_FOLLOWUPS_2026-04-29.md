### 2.1 Partnership-on-paper conversation

> Per our prior conversations about deal structure, can we start the conversation about what this partnership looks like in writing? My suggestion is a phased approach:
>
> - **LOI (Letter of Intent)** in the next 2-3 weeks — non-binding, captures key terms (deal structure, scope, timeline, IP license outline)
> - **MOU (Memorandum of Understanding)** during alpha + refinement (Phase B/C, ~Jun-Jul) — payment milestones, deliverables, IP attribution
> - **Definitive Agreement** during beta + EA prep (Phase D/E, ~Aug-Sep) — full contract, signed before Steam EA launch
>
> Open questions for you:
>
> - Are you comfortable starting with an LOI in the next 2-3 weeks while alpha runs?
> - Should the LOI include language about future 5x-system projects, or scope it to just 5PFH digital for now?
> - Will Modiphius's legal team draft the MOU and Definitive Agreement, or do you want me to bring my own?

### 2.2 Contractor structure conversation

> We've discussed the contractor option in prior conversations. Now that 50/50 net is the working frame, I want to put three concrete scope options on the table for your consideration. Each is a different way to address a real concern, not just a pricing tweak:
>
> - **Frame A — Contractor for the introductory project**: $30-45K up-front + reduced rev share (20-25%). Best when Modiphius wants to capitalize the platform-establishment work for downstream IP integrations.
> - **Frame B — Post-launch support retainer**: monthly retainer (~$3-5K/mo for 6-12 months post-EA, decreasing) + pure 50/50 rev share. Best when build is mine to bear via rev share but Modiphius wants dedicated post-launch availability.
> - **Frame C — Hybrid**: smaller intro fee ($15-20K) + 50/50 + post-launch retainer ($3K/mo × 6mo). Phased commitment.
>
> Multi-project reframe: if you view this build as the foundation for the 5x system across other licensed IPs, the contractor structure is closer to **platform R&D investment** than per-product cost. A retainer also locks in the dev who built the platform for the next IP integration.
>
> I'll send the full forecast doc separately with the math worked out under all three frames (50/50 baseline, break-even tables, scope rationale).
>
> **What I'd like from you**: a directional read on which frame resonates so I can refine the proposal accordingly. No commitment yet — this is a starting-point conversation.

### 2.3 Asset delivery confirmation

> Priority items (from existing ask list §3):

Industry norm for indie closed alphas is a no-NDA gentleman's agreement ("please don't post screenshots / streams of pre-1.0 builds").
