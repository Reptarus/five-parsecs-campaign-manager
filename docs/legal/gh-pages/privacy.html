Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how ReptarusOnIce ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Five Parsecs Campaign Manager application ("App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. If you have any questions, please contact us at [CONTACT EMAIL - TO BE ADDED BEFORE RELEASE].

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Campaign save files — your campaign progress, crew data, and game state
  • App settings — display, audio, gameplay, and accessibility preferences
  • Portrait images — photos you upload for character portraits
  • Tutorial progress — which tutorials you have completed or skipped
  • Legal consent records — timestamps of when you accepted this policy and the EULA
  • Review preferences — whether you have been prompted for an app store review

1.2 Anonymous Gameplay Metrics

The App may collect anonymous, non-personal gameplay metrics to help us improve the App. This data is stored locally on your device only.

If you enable "Share Anonymous Usage Data" in Settings, this anonymous data may be transmitted to our servers in the future. This feature is opt-in and disabled by default. You can change your preference at any time in Settings.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, device ID, IP address, or location through our own systems.

1.3 Platform Store Data

When you purchase the App or expansion content through a third-party storefront (Google Play, Apple App Store, or Steam), that platform manages your personal data related to the transaction. We do not receive or store your payment information or personal account details.

The following platform SDKs are integrated into the App and may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Billing (Android) — processes purchases, subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit (iOS) — processes purchases, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • GodotSteam / Steamworks (PC) — processes purchases and achievements, subject to Valve's Privacy Policy
  • In-App Review API (Android/iOS) — triggers the platform's review prompt; no data is sent to us

2. How We Use Your Information

The data stored on your device is used exclusively to:

  • Operate the App — save your progress, apply your settings, display your content
  • Improve the App — if you opt in to anonymous usage data sharing, we use aggregated metrics to prioritize bug fixes and feature development

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating and improving the App.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

4. Data Retention

  • Local data is retained on your device until you delete it (via Settings or by uninstalling the App)
  • Anonymous usage metrics (if shared) are retained only as long as needed for development prioritization and are not linked to any individual

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — use "Export My Data" in Settings
  • Delete your data — use "Delete All Data" in Settings
  • Control analytics — enable or disable anonymous usage data sharing at any time in Settings
  • Withdraw consent — you may stop using the App at any time

5.1 GDPR (European Union)

If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation. Since we do not collect or process personal data through our servers, these rights are primarily exercisable through the platform stores and through the in-app data management tools described above.

5.2 CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Data Security

All App data is stored locally on your device using standard file system storage. We do not transmit data over the network unless you explicitly opt in to anonymous usage data sharing.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If the policy is updated, you will be notified within the App.
