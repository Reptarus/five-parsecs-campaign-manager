# Chapter 11: Victory Conditions

> **Quick Start** (for tabletop veterans)
> - Choose 1-5 conditions at campaign creation; ANY one met = campaign won
> - 21 types across 5 categories: Duration, Combat, Story, Wealth, Challenge
> - Progress tracked automatically with percentage bars on the Dashboard
> - Custom targets available (e.g., set 50 credits instead of default 100)
> - "Play Forever" option for open-ended sandbox campaigns

## Overview

Victory conditions define when your campaign is "won." You select them during campaign creation, and the app tracks progress throughout your campaign. When any single condition is met, your campaign reaches its conclusion and you can choose to end victoriously or continue playing.

## Choosing Victory Conditions

During campaign creation (Step 1: Configuration), you select **1 to 5** victory conditions. Choosing multiple conditions means your campaign ends when **any one** of them is met — they all progress simultaneously.

### Decision Guidance

- **Single condition**: Focused campaign with a clear goal
- **2-3 conditions**: Balanced approach — whichever happens first wins
- **4-5 conditions**: Faster campaigns since more paths lead to victory
- **Play Forever**: No win condition — play as long as you want

## Victory Categories

### Duration (5 conditions)

Time-based goals measured in campaign turns:

| Condition | Default Target | Typical Playtime |
|-----------|---------------|------------------|
| 20 Turns | 20 turns | 4-6 hours |
| 50 Turns | 50 turns | 10-15 hours |
| 100 Turns | 100 turns | 20-30 hours |
| Play Forever | None | Unlimited |
| Custom Duration | Player-set (5-200) | Variable |

### Combat (5 conditions)

Battle-focused achievements:

| Condition | Default Target | Typical Playtime |
|-----------|---------------|------------------|
| Battles Won | 30 battles | 8-12 hours |
| Enemies Defeated | 100 enemies | 8-12 hours |
| Rivals Defeated | 3 rivals eliminated | 6-10 hours |
| Quests Completed | 3 quest chains | 8-12 hours |
| Boss Battle | 1 boss defeated | 6-10 hours |

### Story (3 conditions)

Narrative-driven goals:

| Condition | Default Target |
|-----------|---------------|
| Story Points | 20 accumulated |
| Quests Completed | 5 full chains |
| Story Finale | 1 campaign story completion |

### Wealth (2 conditions)

Economic achievements:

| Condition | Default Target |
|-----------|---------------|
| Credits Accumulated | 100 total credits earned |
| Fame | 10 reputation points |

### Challenge (2 conditions)

Difficulty-based achievements for experienced players:

| Condition | Default Target | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Flawless Campaign | 20 turns with zero deaths | Very difficult |
| Ironman | 50 turns with permadeath | No reloading saves |

## Custom Targets

For most conditions, you can adjust the target within limits:

| Condition Type | Min | Max |
|---------------|-----|-----|
| Turns | 5 | 200 |
| Credits | 25 | 1,000 |
| Battles | 5 | 100 |
| Enemies | 10 | 500 |
| Reputation | 3 | 50 |

This lets you create campaigns of any length or difficulty.

## Tracking Progress

### Dashboard Display

The Campaign Dashboard shows all selected conditions with:

- **Progress bars** showing percentage complete (0-100%)
- **Milestone markers** at 25%, 50%, and 75%
- **Current value** vs. target value
- **Highlighted condition** — the one closest to completion

### Victory Progress Screen

A dedicated screen (accessible from the Dashboard) shows detailed breakdowns:

- Per-condition progress history
- Rate of progress (how many turns until estimated victory)
- Comparison between conditions

## Winning and Ending

When any condition reaches 100%:

1. The app announces your victory at the End Phase
2. You see a summary of your campaign's achievements
3. You can choose to:
   - **End the campaign** — Final stats recorded, campaign archived in Legacy system
   - **Continue playing** — Keep going past your victory (the condition stays met)

### Legacy System

Completed campaigns are archived with:
- Final crew roster and stats
- Campaign statistics (turns played, battles won, credits earned)
- Notable achievements
- Stars of Story earned (carry over to future campaigns)
