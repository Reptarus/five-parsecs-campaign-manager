# Chapter 3: The Campaign Dashboard

> **Quick Start** (for tabletop veterans)
> - The Dashboard is your hub between turns — shows crew, credits, turn number, and phase progress
> - Access crew management, equipment, ship details, and save/load from here
> - Victory progress is tracked with progress bars for each selected condition
> - The Campaign Journal logs everything that happens each turn

## Overview

The Campaign Dashboard is your central command screen. It shows the current state of your campaign at a glance and provides access to all management functions. You'll return here between turns and can access it during certain phases.

## Reading the Dashboard

### Campaign Header

At the top of the dashboard, you'll see:

- **Campaign Name** — The name you chose during creation
- **Turn Number** — How many turns you've completed
- **Current Phase** — Which phase of the campaign turn you're in
- **Credits** — Your available funds

### Crew Overview

The crew section shows your current roster:

- **Captain** — Displayed prominently with full stats
- **Crew Members** — Each member shown with:
  - Name and species
  - Core stats (Combat, Reaction, Speed, Toughness, Savvy, Luck)
  - Current health status
  - Equipped weapon and armor
  - Any active injuries or conditions

Crew members with injuries show recovery time remaining. Members who are incapacitated appear dimmed.

### Resources Panel

Your current resources at a glance:

| Resource | What It Means |
|----------|--------------|
| **Credits** | Spending money for upkeep, equipment, repairs |
| **Story Points** | Narrative currency from story events |
| **Reputation** | Your crew's fame/infamy in the galaxy |
| **Patrons** | Number of active patron relationships |
| **Rivals** | Number of active rival enemies |
| **Rumors** | Available leads for quests and missions |

### Victory Progress

If you selected victory conditions during campaign creation, the dashboard shows progress bars for each:

- **Percentage complete** (0-100%) for each condition
- **Milestone markers** at 25%, 50%, and 75%
- The condition closest to completion is highlighted

When any condition reaches 100%, your campaign is won! See {{chapter:11}} for details.

## Dashboard Actions

From the dashboard, you can access several management screens:

### Start Next Turn

The primary action button launches the Campaign Turn Controller to begin your next turn. This takes you through the 9-phase turn sequence. See {{chapter:04}}.

### Crew Management

Opens the full crew management screen where you can:

- View detailed character sheets for each crew member
- Reassign equipment between crew members
- Review skills, abilities, and advancement history
- Check injury status and recovery progress

See {{chapter:09}} for details on character management.

### Equipment

Opens your equipment inventory:

- View all owned weapons, armor, and items
- Check equipment in the ship stash (unequipped gear)
- Compare equipment stats side-by-side
- Assign or swap equipment between crew members

See {{chapter:10}} for equipment details.

### Ship

Opens ship management:

- View ship stats (hull, armor, modifications)
- Check cargo space and stash contents
- Review ship debt and payment status
- Manage ship upgrades and repairs

### Save / Load

Opens the save/load screen:

- **Save** your current campaign
- **Load** a different campaign
- **Export** campaign data to a file
- **Import** a campaign file
- View auto-save history

The app auto-saves at key moments (start of each turn, after battles), but manual saves let you create checkpoint saves before risky decisions. See {{chapter:14}}.

### Campaign Journal

The Campaign Journal is an automatic log of everything that happens in your campaign:

- Turn-by-turn timeline of events
- Battle summaries with outcomes
- Crew changes (injuries, deaths, recruitment)
- Financial transactions
- Story events and quest progress

The journal is useful for reviewing what happened in previous turns, especially if you're returning to a campaign after a break.

## Navigation Tips

- Use the **Back** button to return to the Main Menu
- The **?** help button opens context-sensitive help for the current screen
- Phase panels in the Turn Controller advance automatically when you complete each phase
- You can access crew and equipment management during certain phases (World Phase, Trading Phase)
