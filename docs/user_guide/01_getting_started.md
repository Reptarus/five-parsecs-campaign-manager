# Chapter 1: Getting Started

> **Quick Start** (for tabletop veterans)
> - Launch the app and you'll see the Main Menu with 7 buttons
> - Hit **New Campaign** to start the 7-step creation wizard
> - Hit **Continue** to resume your last campaign (appears only if a save exists)
> - Hit **Load Campaign** to browse saved campaigns or import from file
> - The **Library** button opens this help system

## Overview

The Five Parsecs Campaign Manager is a companion app for the *Five Parsecs from Home* tabletop game. It handles campaign management, dice rolling, combat tracking, and turn-by-turn bookkeeping — letting you focus on moving miniatures and making decisions.

When you first launch the app, you'll arrive at the **Main Menu**. From here, you can start a new campaign, continue an existing one, or access other features.

## The Main Menu

The Main Menu is your home screen. It presents the following options:

### Continue

Resumes your most recent campaign exactly where you left off. This button only appears if you have an active campaign in progress.

If you want to switch to a different saved campaign, use **Load Campaign** instead.

### New Campaign

Launches the **Campaign Creation Wizard** — a 7-step guided process that walks you through setting up a new campaign. You'll configure your campaign type, create your captain, recruit your crew, generate starting equipment, choose a ship, generate your first world, and review everything before starting.

See {{chapter:02}} for the full walkthrough.

### Load Campaign

Opens the campaign browser where you can:
- **Load** a previously saved campaign from the list
- **Import** a campaign file from your computer (`.save` or `.json` format)

Each saved campaign shows its name and the date it was last played. Select one and you'll jump straight into the campaign turn controller.

### Bug Hunt

Launches the **Bug Hunt** game mode — a standalone military campaign variant from the Compendium expansion. Bug Hunt uses a simplified 3-stage turn (Special Assignments, Mission, Post-Battle) with squad-based gameplay instead of the standard 9-phase campaign turn.

Bug Hunt requires the Compendium DLC. See {{chapter:12}} for details.

### Battle Simulator

A standalone battle testing mode (coming in a future update). This will let you set up custom battles outside of a campaign for practice or experimentation.

### Options

Opens the Settings screen where you can adjust:
- **Visual theme** (dark/light modes)
- **Text size** for readability
- **Reduced animation** for accessibility
- **Other preferences**

See {{chapter:15}} for all available settings.

### Library

Opens the **User Guide** — the in-app version of the document you're reading now. You can browse chapters, search for topics, and access context-sensitive help from any screen in the app.

## Your First Campaign

Ready to play? Here's how to get started:

1. **Click "New Campaign"** on the Main Menu
2. **Follow the 7-step wizard** (detailed in {{chapter:02}}):
   - Step 1: Name your campaign and choose a campaign type
   - Step 2: Create your captain (the crew leader)
   - Step 3: Recruit your starting crew (4-6 members total)
   - Step 4: Generate starting equipment
   - Step 5: Choose your ship
   - Step 6: Generate your first world
   - Step 7: Review and confirm
3. **Start playing** — the app will launch the Campaign Turn Controller and walk you through your first turn

### What You'll Need

- A copy of the *Five Parsecs from Home* rulebook (for reference during play)
- Miniatures and a playing surface (for tabletop battles)
- Dice (d6, d10, d100) — or use the app's built-in dice roller

The app can handle all dice rolling digitally, but many players prefer rolling physical dice and entering the results manually. You can switch between automatic and manual dice modes at any time.

## Understanding the Screen Layout

The app uses a **Deep Space** visual theme with a dark navy background. Key visual cues:

- **Cards** (raised panels with borders) group related information together
- **Cyan highlights** indicate focused or selected elements
- **Green text** indicates success or positive outcomes
- **Orange text** indicates warnings or costs
- **Red text** indicates danger, errors, or negative outcomes

### Navigation

The app uses a screen-based navigation system. You'll move between screens as you progress through campaign phases. Key navigation patterns:

- **Back buttons** return you to the previous screen
- **Phase panels** advance automatically as you complete each campaign phase
- **The Campaign Dashboard** is your hub between turns — you can access crew management, equipment, ship details, and more from there

## Tips for New Players

- **Save often.** The app auto-saves at key points, but you can also save manually from the Campaign Dashboard. See {{chapter:14}}.
- **Read the phase instructions.** Each campaign phase shows guidance text explaining what you need to do.
- **Use the help buttons.** Look for the **?** icon on screens — it opens context-sensitive help for that specific feature.
- **Don't worry about mistakes.** You can always load a previous save if something goes wrong.
- **The battle system is a companion, not a simulator.** It gives you text instructions to execute on your physical tabletop. You still need miniatures, terrain, and dice for battles (though the app can roll dice for you).
