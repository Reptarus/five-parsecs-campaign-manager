# Five Parsecs Campaign Manager — User Guide

Welcome to the **Five Parsecs Campaign Manager**, a digital companion app for the *Five Parsecs from Home* tabletop game by Modiphius Entertainment. This app helps you manage your campaign, track your crew, roll dice, and run battles — all from one screen.

Whether you're a seasoned Five Parsecs veteran or picking up the game for the first time, this guide will walk you through every feature of the app.

## About Five Parsecs from Home

*Five Parsecs from Home* is a solo adventure wargame by Ivan Sorensen, published by Modiphius Entertainment. You lead a ragtag crew of spacers across the galaxy, taking on jobs, fighting enemies, and trying to survive long enough to achieve your goals. The physical game uses miniatures, dice, and a rulebook — this app handles the bookkeeping so you can focus on the adventure.

This companion app is not a replacement for the rulebook. You'll still need your copy of *Five Parsecs from Home* (and optionally the *Compendium*) to play. The app manages your campaign data, rolls dice, tracks combat, and walks you through each turn phase.
