## 3. Game Description

Provide both a short and a long description to fit different content formats.

### 3.1. Short Description (Elevator Pitch)

> Five Parsecs Campaign Manager is a digital companion for the acclaimed solo tabletop game, *Five Parsecs From Home*. It faithfully translates the game's complex rules into a seamless digital experience, managing everything from character creation and combat to campaign events and story progression. Dive into a procedurally generated galaxy, lead your crew of sci-fi adventurers, and create your own emergent stories of grit and survival.

### 3.2. Long Description

> Go beyond the tabletop with the Five Parsecs Campaign Manager, the definitive digital adaptation of Modiphius's *Five Parsecs From Home*. Designed for both veterans and newcomers to the solo RPG experience, this application handles all the bookkeeping, letting you focus on the strategic decisions and narrative of your campaign.
>
> Create a unique crew of characters from a variety of species, backgrounds, and motivations. Equip them with a vast arsenal of weapons, armor, and gear. Undertake a huge variety of procedurally generated missions, from tense stealth infiltrations to chaotic street fights and desperate salvage jobs. The Campaign Manager tracks every detail, from enemy encounters and battle resolutions to post-mission injuries and campaign-spanning rivalries.
>
> With the optional Compendium DLC, you can expand your universe even further with psionic characters, new playable species, and a host of advanced rules that add even more depth and challenge to your galactic adventures.

## 4. Features List

Use a bulleted list to highlight the key features.

-   **Faithful Adaptation:** A complete digital implementation of the *Five Parsecs From Home* core rules.
-   **Full Campaign Management:** Automates character creation, combat resolution, enemy generation, and all campaign phases.
-   **Procedurally Generated Universe:** Every campaign is unique, with procedurally generated worlds, missions, and events.
-   **Deep Character Progression:** Watch your crew grow from rookies to hardened veterans with new skills, equipment, and story-driven traits.
-   **Emergent Narrative:** Create your own stories through the dynamic and interconnected systems of the game.
-   **Optional Compendium DLC:** Massively expand the game with psionics, new species, advanced missions, and more.
