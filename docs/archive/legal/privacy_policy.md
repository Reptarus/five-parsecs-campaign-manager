# Privacy Policy for Five Parsecs Campaign Manager

**Last Updated:** [Date of Release]

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how [Your Name/Studio Name] ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our application, Five Parsecs Campaign Manager (the "Game").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy is intended to be straightforward and clear. If you have any questions, please contact us at [privacy@yourgame.com].

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect a minimal amount of data necessary to operate and improve the Game.

### 2.1. In-Game Bug & Crash Reports (Optional)

If you choose to submit a bug report or if the Game experiences a crash, we may collect the following **non-personal, anonymous** information:

-   **Game State Data**: The current scene, campaign turn, and other non-personal gameplay variables.
-   **Device and Game Version**: The type of device you are using (e.g., "Windows", "Android") and the version of the Game you are running.
-   **Technical Logs**: A snippet of the console log and/or a stack trace in the event of a crash.
-   **Screenshots and Save Games (Optional)**: If you explicitly grant permission, we will collect a screenshot and/or a copy of your save file to help us reproduce the issue.

**We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII)** such as your name, email address, device ID, or IP address through this system.

### 2.2. Platform-Specific Data (Handled by Storefronts)

When you purchase the Game or any DLC through a third-party storefront (like Steam, Google Play, or the Apple App Store), that storefront manages your personal data related to the transaction. We do not receive or store your payment information or personal account details. We only receive anonymous, aggregated sales data.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The anonymous information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

-   **To Improve the Game**: We analyze bug reports and crash data to identify, understand, and fix issues, thereby improving the stability and quality of the Game.
-   **To Prioritize Development**: Understanding what parts of the Game are causing issues helps us prioritize our development efforts.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

The anonymous data collected may be processed by the following third-party services for the sole purpose of issue tracking:

-   **GitHub**: Bug reports may be converted into issues in a private GitHub repository for tracking and resolution.
-   **Cloud Storage (e.g., Google Cloud, AWS)**: Screenshots and save files may be temporarily stored in a private, secure cloud storage bucket.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain the anonymous bug and crash report data only as long as it is necessary to resolve the reported issue. Once an issue is resolved and closed, the associated data (especially screenshots and save files) may be deleted.

## 6. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personally identifiable information, the typical rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of personal data do not apply. You have the right to not submit bug or crash reports. The choice is always yours.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from them.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the Game or on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
