## Surface 1: Community announcement post

**Help shape the digital companion for Five Parsecs From Home**

We are building a digital companion app for Five Parsecs From Home, and we are looking for tabletop players to help us get it right.

> [Modiphius endorsement slot: Chris and Gavin add Modiphius's "officially partnered / supported" language here.]

**What it is.** A companion for your Five Parsecs campaigns. It runs your campaign turns, assists you through battles, keeps track of your crew and characters, and brings the game's events and tables to life on screen. It is built to sit alongside your physical books and your table, not to replace them: a digital aide for the solo and small-group play the game is loved for.

**What we are looking for.** Players who know Five Parsecs at the table and want a hand in shaping its digital version. Specifically:

- You have played Five Parsecs From Home on the tabletop (any mode).
- You are happy to give blunt, honest feedback. We want to hear what is broken and what is missing, not only what is nice.
- You are patient with rough edges. This is an early alpha. It works, but it is not polished yet.
- You can run the app a couple of times a week over roughly six weeks.
- You are happy to give feedback through a few channels: email and short surveys for structured input, and Discord for the fast day-to-day back-and-forth. Discord is the one closest to the ground, and it is where you will have a direct line to me, the developer. No scheduled meetings to attend.

**What it is not, yet.** This is a closed alpha on Windows. It is not finished, it is not public, and we ask testers to keep screenshots and recordings off social media until we open things up later. If you are looking for a polished, finished product, this is not that moment. If you want to help build it, this is exactly that moment.

**Where it is going.** Feedback from this alpha shapes the build we take to Steam Early Access later this year. Testers who see the program through are credited as playtesters.

**How to sign up.** Fill out the short form here: [SIGNUP FORM LINK]. It takes a few minutes and helps us put together a focused group of testers. We will be in touch with everyone we bring into the alpha.

Thank you for helping us make this the digital companion Five Parsecs deserves.
