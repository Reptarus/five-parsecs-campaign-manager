## Phase-2-closing clauses to watch for (defensive checklist)

When the boilerplate sample arrives, scan for:

- **Engine ownership**: Does it transfer the GDScript platform code to Modiphius? Block this.
- **Exclusivity**: Does it prevent Elijah from working on other IPs through anyone else during the contract term? Modify or block.
- **IP-grab language**: Can Modiphius take the platform code for other projects without Elijah's involvement? Block.
- **Non-compete**: Any clauses preventing parallel work for other tabletop publishers? Block or scope-limit.
- **ROFR (right of first refusal)**: Does Modiphius get ROFR on Phase 2 terms before Phase 2 is negotiated? Block.
- **Termination**: What happens to Elijah's deliverables if Modiphius terminates? Pro-rata recoupment treatment? IP reversion?
- **Audit rights**: Does Elijah have rights to audit Modiphius's reporting on recoupment? Important for any structure where Modiphius controls the revenue counting.
