## 1. Executive Summary

Five Parsecs Campaign Manager is a fully built, rules-accurate digital companion for the entire Five Parsecs From Home product line. The app covers all four game modes (Core Campaign, Bug Hunt, Planetfall, Tactics), implements 170 out of 170 core mechanics, and has over 925 verified data values extracted directly from the rulebooks. With 1,020+ source files, tri-platform store adapters (Steam, iOS, Android), and a complete legal/DLC infrastructure already in place, the product is ready for commercial release. This proposal outlines three monetization models, revenue forecasts based on the existing Five Parsecs customer base, and a recommended revenue split structure. The core thesis: this is not just a companion app -- it is the digital version of Five Parsecs, and should be priced and positioned accordingly.

## 5. Model C: Hybrid (Recommended)

Combine the strengths of both models. Offer multiple paths to unlock the full app, capturing both "pay once" and "subscribe" customer segments.

### Pricing Structure

| Option | Price | Access |
|--------|------:|--------|
| Free Tier | $0 | Character creation, campaign demo (3 turns), Compendium browser |
| One-Time Unlock | $14.99 - $19.99 | Full Core Campaign, permanent |
| Monthly Subscription | $3.99/mo | Full Core Campaign + ongoing updates |
| Annual Subscription | $29.99/yr | Same as monthly, ~37% discount |
| DLC Packs | $2.99 - $7.99 each | Compendium expansions, Bug Hunt, Planetfall, Tactics |

*One-time unlock buyers still purchase DLC packs separately. Subscribers get Core Campaign only -- DLC packs are always separate one-time purchases regardless of payment method.*

## 6. Revenue Split Options

All figures shown **after platform fees** (using blended 20% rate). Three tiers for discussion.

### Tier Definitions

| Tier | Modiphius Share | Developer Share | Rationale |
|------|:-:|:-:|-----------|
| **Favorable** | 5 - 10% | 90 - 95% | Modiphius provides IP license and store accounts only. Developer funds all development, marketing, and support |
| **Standard** | 15 - 20% | 80 - 85% | Modiphius provides IP license, store accounts, art assets, marketing support, and community access |
| **Publisher-Level** | 25 - 30% | 70 - 75% | Modiphius acts as full publisher: funds marketing, provides QA resources, handles customer support, co-manages store listings |

- The full post-platform revenue is split between two parties, not three
- Both sides benefit from cleaner economics and simpler contracts

## 7. Steam "Digital Version" Premium Pricing

Chris described the vision as "the digital version of Five Parsecs" -- not a companion app. This framing supports premium pricing.

### Price Justification

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Book PDF price** | Core Rules PDF is $27.62. The app delivers an interactive, automated version of that same content |
| **App scope** | 170/170 mechanics, 925+ verified values, 4 game modes, full campaign lifecycle |
| **Development investment** | 1,020+ GDScript files, 9 months of development, 57+ working sessions |
| **Comparable digital tabletop tools** | Gloomhaven Digital ($34.99), Talisman Digital ($6.99 + $100+ DLC), Tabletop Simulator ($19.99) |

A base price of **$14.99-$19.99** is well below the book PDF price and represents strong value for a fully interactive digital implementation.

### Platform-Specific Pricing Strategy

| Platform | Base Price | Rationale |
|----------|----------:|-----------|
| Steam | $19.99 | Higher-value audience, "digital version" positioning, 30% platform cut requires higher price to maintain revenue |
| iOS / Android | $14.99 (or free + IAP) | Mobile users expect lower prices; 15% platform fee compensates for the lower price point |
| Subscription (mobile) | $3.99/mo | Mobile-native payment model, 15% fee makes this the highest-margin channel |

**Steam DLC bundle pricing**: Offer the Compendium Bundle at $14.99 on Steam (same as mobile), but position the base app + all DLC as a "Complete Edition" at $29.99-$34.99 for customers who want everything upfront.

## 8. Five Leagues Expansion Economics

The framework built for Five Parsecs is designed to support additional rulesets. Five Leagues From the Borderlands is the confirmed next target.

### Conversion Timeline & Cost

| Factor | Estimate |
|--------|----------|
| **Five Leagues audience** | 30,000 - 35,000 (same size as Five Parsecs) |
| **Framework reuse** | Campaign engine, battle system, UI components, store/DLC infrastructure, legal stack -- all reusable |
| **Estimated conversion time** | 1-2 weeks for core rules, 3-4 weeks for full product parity |
| **New code required** | Primarily data files (JSON), new UI skins/themes, rules-specific logic |
| **Incremental development cost** | ~5-10% of the original Five Parsecs build effort |

### Revenue Impact

Five Leagues effectively doubles the addressable market (30K + 30K = 60K book buyers) with minimal incremental development cost.

| Model | Five Parsecs Only (Yr 1) | + Five Leagues (Yr 2) | Combined Two-Year |
|-------|--:|--:|--:|
| Hybrid Moderate | $89,611 | +$89,611 | **$179,222** |
| Hybrid Optimistic | $137,585 | +$137,585 | **$275,170** |

*Assumes Five Leagues achieves the same conversion rates as Five Parsecs. Actual rates may be higher due to established app reputation and cross-promotion.*

### The Platform Value Argument

This is not a single-product deal. The architecture supports:

1. **Five Parsecs From Home** (built, ready to ship)
2. **Five Leagues From the Borderlands** (1-2 week conversion)
3. **Five Klicks From the Zone** (same engine, same conversion timeline)
4. **Future Modiphius solo/coop titles** using similar campaign structures

Each new title added to the platform increases subscriber value (for the subscription model) and creates cross-sell opportunities. The licensing terms should reflect this long-term platform value.

### Licensing Structure Options

| Option | Structure | Best For |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| **Per-title license** | Fixed annual fee per ruleset (e.g., $2,000-$5,000/year per game) | Predictable costs, caps Modiphius's risk |
| **Unified revenue share** | Same percentage split applies to all titles on the platform | Simplest contract, scales naturally |
| **Tiered share** | Lower rate for developer-funded titles, higher rate if Modiphius co-funds | Incentivizes Modiphius investment in marketing/assets |

**Recommendation**: Unified revenue share. One contract covers the platform, and each new game added benefits both parties without renegotiation.

## 9. Recommendation

### Recommended Model: Hybrid (Model C)

The hybrid model maximizes revenue by capturing both one-time purchasers and subscribers, while the free tier drives App Store discovery and word-of-mouth. Specific pricing:

- **Free tier**: Character creation + 3-turn campaign demo
- **One-time unlock**: $14.99 (mobile) / $19.99 (Steam)
- **Monthly subscription**: $3.99/month (mobile only)
- **Annual subscription**: $29.99/year (mobile only)
- **DLC packs**: $2.99-$7.99 each (all platforms)
- **Steam Complete Edition**: $29.99-$34.99 (base + all DLC)

### Recommended Split: Standard (15-20%)

A **15-20% share to Modiphius** is the recommended starting point, based on:

1. **Developer-funded product**: The entire application -- 1,020+ files, 57+ development sessions, tri-platform infrastructure -- was built and funded entirely by the developer prior to any partnership agreement
2. **No upstream licensor**: Unlike Fallout 2d20, the full post-platform revenue is split between two parties. 15-20% of a clean split is more valuable than 15-20% of a three-way split
3. **Modiphius contributions**: IP license, publisher store accounts, art assets, community access, and marketing support justify a meaningful share
4. **Industry benchmarks**: Standard IP licensing for digital adaptations of tabletop games typically ranges from 10-25%, depending on publisher involvement
5. **Growth incentive**: The split should be attractive enough that Modiphius is motivated to actively promote the app, share assets, and prioritize Five Leagues conversion

### Proposed Terms

| Term | Proposal |
|------|----------|
| **Revenue share** | 15% to Modiphius / 85% to Developer (net of platform fees) |
| **Scope** | All Five Parsecs From Home digital products (base + all DLC + expansions) |
| **Five Leagues** | Same terms, activated when Five Leagues module ships |
| **Future titles** | Same terms apply to any additional ruleset added to the platform |
| **Minimum guarantee** | None in Year 1 (performance-based only) |
| **Term length** | 3 years with annual renewal, 90-day termination notice |
| **Audit rights** | Both parties may audit revenue reports annually |
| **Marketing commitment** | Modiphius includes app in Five Parsecs marketing materials, newsletter mentions, store page cross-links |
