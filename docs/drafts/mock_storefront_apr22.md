### Long Description ("About This Game" Section)

The following uses Steam's supported formatting (headers, bold, lists, horizontal rules).

---

**THE GALAXY IS YOURS**

Five Parsecs From Home: Digital Edition is the definitive digital experience for Ivan Sorensen's acclaimed solo adventure wargame, published by Modiphius Entertainment. Every rule faithfully translated. Every table accurately implemented. Every expansion included or available as DLC. Build your crew, explore a procedurally generated galaxy, and create emergent stories of grit, survival, and unlikely heroism.

This is not a simplified adaptation. This is the complete Five Parsecs From Home -- all 925+ data values verified against the Core Rulebook and Compendium. If it's in the book, it's in the app.

---

**YOUR CREW, YOUR STORY**

Assemble a crew from 10+ species -- Humans, K'Erin warriors, enigmatic Precursors, feral outcasts, hulking bruisers, soulless constructs, and more. Every character has a background, a motivation, and a class drawn from the Core Rules tables. Watch them grow from rookies to hardened veterans across dozens of campaign turns, earning experience, collecting gear, building rivalries, and forging their legend.

---

**FOUR COMPLETE GAME MODES**

- **Core Campaign** -- The full 9-phase campaign turn loop. Story events, travel, upkeep, patron jobs, tactical missions, post-battle resolution, advancement, trading, and retirement checks. Everything from the Core Rulebook, faithfully implemented.

- **Bug Hunt** -- Standalone military operations from the Compendium. Lead a squad of soldiers against alien infestations in a streamlined 3-stage turn cycle. No ship, no patrons -- just your grunts against the swarm.

- **Planetfall** -- Colony-building campaign from the Compendium. Establish and defend a settlement across an 18-step turn flow covering scouting, colony events, building, research, and defending against escalating threats.

- **Tactics** -- Points-based army building and operational campaigns from the Tactics expansion. Build armies from 14 species lists, field vehicles and veteran units, and fight through linked scenarios.

---

**A DIGITAL GM SCREEN, NOT A VIDEO GAME**

Five Parsecs From Home: Digital Edition is designed to enhance your tabletop experience, not replace it. Three tracking modes let you play the way you want:

- **Log Only** -- Record outcomes you resolve yourself at the table
- **Assisted** -- The app suggests outcomes; you make the final call
- **Full Oracle** -- Let the app handle all dice rolls, table lookups, and resolution

The tactical battle companion provides 26 reference panels in a tabbed layout: dice dashboard, combat calculator, weapon tables, activation tracker, round management, and a procedurally generated battlefield map with terrain from 7 Compendium themes.

---

**PROCEDURALLY GENERATED, ENDLESSLY REPLAYABLE**

Every campaign is unique. Worlds are generated with traits that affect terrain, combat, and events. Missions are drawn from patron jobs, rival encounters, and story-driven quest chains. Post-battle consequences cascade through your crew -- injuries, equipment damage, new rivals, unexpected windfalls. The Story Track system weaves a 7-event narrative arc through your campaign, complete with an evidence mechanic and escalating stakes.

---

**925+ DATA VALUES. ZERO FABRICATION.**

Every stat, every cost, every table range, every probability in this app comes directly from the Five Parsecs From Home rulebooks. We verified 925+ individual data values against the Core Rulebook and Compendium, and 108 additional values against the Tactics expansion. When the book says Infantry Laser range is 30", the app says 30". No balancing. No "improvements." The books define the game; the app implements it faithfully.

---

**BUILT FOR ACCESSIBILITY**

- 4 colorblind modes (Protanopia, Deuteranopia, Tritanopia, Achromatopsia)
- High contrast Deep Space theme
- Reduced motion option for all animations
- Adjustable font sizing (Small / Normal / Large)
- Full keyboard navigation
- Touch-friendly controls (48px+ touch targets) for Steam Deck and tablet play

---

### Key Features (Bulleted List for Steam Sidebar)

1. **Complete Core Rules Implementation** -- All 170 game mechanics from the Five Parsecs From Home Core Rulebook, faithfully digitized
2. **Four Game Modes** -- Core Campaign, Bug Hunt, Planetfall, and Tactics -- the entire Five Parsecs product line in one app
3. **10+ Playable Species** -- Humans, K'Erin, Precursors, Ferals, Hulkers, Soulless, Engineers, Swift, and Compendium species (Krag, Skulker) with unique gameplay traits
4. **9-Phase Campaign Turns** -- Story, Travel, Upkeep, Mission, Post-Mission, Advancement, Trading, Character Events, and Retirement -- every phase fully interactive
5. **Procedural Battlefield Generation** -- 7 terrain themes, 10 world trait modifications, seeded RNG for reproducible maps
6. **Tactical Battle Companion** -- 26-panel digital GM screen with dice, combat calculator, weapon tables, and activation tracking
7. **Full Save/Load System** -- Per-turn auto-save, multiple save slots, campaign type auto-detection
8. **Deep Compendium Support** -- Psionics, progressive difficulty, elite enemies, expanded missions, stealth ops, salvage jobs, and more via optional DLC packs
9. **Red & Black Zone Endgame** -- High-stakes missions for veteran crews with exclusive rewards and escalating threats

## Appendix: Competitive Landscape

For context, here are comparable tabletop-to-digital products on Steam and how they are positioned:

| Title | Price | Tags | Reviews | Notes |
|-------|-------|------|---------|-------|
| Gloomhaven (Digital) | $19.99 | RPG, Strategy, Tabletop, Turn-Based | Very Positive (12K+) | Full digital adaptation, not companion. Different: it IS the tactical combat. |
| Sentinels of the Multiverse | $9.99 | Card Game, Tabletop, Strategy | Very Positive (3K+) | Complete digital version of card game. Comparable positioning. |
| Talisman: Digital Edition | $6.99 | Board Game, RPG, Tabletop | Mixed (3K+) | Digital board game. Heavy DLC model (30+ DLC packs). |

**Our positioning**: We are closest to Sentinels of the Multiverse or One Deck Dungeon in model -- a faithful digital version of a specific tabletop game -- but with significantly more depth and content (4 game modes, 170+ mechanics, full campaign persistence). The $14.99 price point sits comfortably between the $9.99 lightweight adaptations and the $19.99 full-featured titles.
